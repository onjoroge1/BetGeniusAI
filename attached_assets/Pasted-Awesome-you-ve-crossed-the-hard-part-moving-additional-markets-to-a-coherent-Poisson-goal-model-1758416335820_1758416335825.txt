# Robustness tweaks (small but important)

* **Normalize 1X2** defensively:

  ```py
  s = max(1e-12, pH + pD + pA)
  pH, pD, pA = pH/s, pD/s, pA/s
  pH = min(max(pH, 1e-6), 1-1e-6); pA = min(max(pA, 1e-6), 1-1e-6); pD = 1 - pH - pA
  ```
* **Adaptive grid size**: choose MAX so that Poisson tail mass is tiny.
  Keep extending MAX until `1 - sum_{k=0..MAX} pmf(k, λ)` < `1e-8` for both teams (typically MAX 8–12).
* **Cache factorials / pmf rows** by `(λ rounded to 2 dp, MAX)` to shave a few ms when cron hits many matches.

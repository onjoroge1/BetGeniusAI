Below is a World Cup–specific data and model preparation blueprint tailored to SnapBet’s current V2 architecture, WhatsApp distribution strategy, and long-term valuation goals. Executive Summary (Why WC Is Different)

The World Cup breaks most club-football assumptions:
	•	Small sample sizes
	•	Cross-league player combinations
	•	Asymmetric information (injuries, politics, fatigue, weather, travel)
	•	Heavy public money → distorted odds
	•	Slower model correction cycles

Your advantage will come from pre-tournament historical learning + fast in-tournament adaptation.

⸻

1️⃣ Core Historical Match Data (Mandatory)

You should start ingesting international-only data, not club data repurposed.

Scope
	•	World Cups (2002–2022)
	•	Continental tournaments:
	•	AFCON
	•	EURO
	•	Copa América
	•	Asian Cup
	•	WC Qualifiers (last 3 cycles minimum)
	•	International friendlies (weighted lower)

Data Points
	•	Final score
	•	HT score
	•	Goal timings
	•	Cards
	•	Penalties
	•	Extra time / shootouts
	•	Match stage (group, R16, QF, etc.)

Why: Tournament dynamics are structurally different—late goals, risk tolerance, and game theory matter.

⸻

2️⃣ Player-Level International Context (Critical Upgrade)

This is where most competitors fall short.

Player Data (International Context Only)
	•	Caps
	•	Goals / assists for country
	•	Minutes played in last 12 months
	•	Tournament experience count
	•	Club minutes (fatigue proxy)
	•	Travel distance to host country
	•	Injury history at international level

Derived Signals
	•	“National Team Chemistry Index”
	•	% of starting XI who’ve played ≥10 matches together
	•	Avg caps per starting XI
	•	Club dispersion index (how many clubs represented)

Why: Club form ≠ international cohesion.

⸻

3️⃣ Squad Stability & Call-Up Volatility

Track who is called up and when.

Features
	•	Squad announcement date
	•	Last-minute call-ups
	•	Withdrawals due to injury/politics
	•	Manager tenure length
	•	% of squad new vs previous tournament

Why: Stability is one of the strongest WC predictors.

⸻

4️⃣ Tournament-Specific Tactical Profiles

You should create international tactical fingerprints, separate from club play.

Team-Level Metrics
	•	Avg possession (tournament-only)
	•	Pressing intensity proxy (PPDA when available)
	•	Shots conceded per match
	•	Set-piece goal dependency
	•	Defensive block depth

Derived Features
	•	“Tournament Pragmatism Score”
	•	“Early-Goal Protection Index”
	•	“Must-Win Aggression Multiplier”

⸻

5️⃣ Market & Odds Behavior (Massive Edge)

This should already align with your CLV work.

Collect
	•	Opening odds
	•	Closing odds
	•	Line movement velocity
	•	Volume proxies (when available)
	•	Favorite vs underdog performance by stage

Special Flags
	•	Host nation bias
	•	Big-name bias
	•	Elimination match bias
	•	Dead-rubber distortion (already qualified teams)

Why: WC markets are emotional and slow to correct.

⸻

6️⃣ Environmental & Logistics Data (Underrated)

World Cups are travel and climate tournaments.

Track
	•	Match city
	•	Temperature
	•	Humidity
	•	Altitude
	•	Rest days since last match
	•	Travel distance between matches
	•	Kickoff local time

Derived Signals
	•	Heat stress index
	•	Altitude mismatch penalty
	•	Recovery disadvantage score

7️⃣ Tournament Psychology Signals (Non-Obvious, High Value)

You won’t find this cleanly in APIs.

Data You Should Encode
	•	Must-win vs can-draw classification
	•	Rivalry matches
	•	Historical WC elimination trauma
	•	Penalty shootout history
	•	Media pressure index (proxy via past knockouts)

Why: WC matches are psychological contests.

⸻

8️⃣ Model Strategy: How to Train This Properly

Phase 1 – Pre-Tournament
	•	Train on:
	•	Past WCs
	•	Continental tournaments
	•	Objective:
	•	Outcome probabilities
	•	Low-variance markets (BTTS, Under, Double Chance)

Phase 2 – Early Group Stage
	•	Rapid Bayesian updates
	•	Downweight historical priors after Matchday 2
	•	Introduce:
	•	Tournament-specific form embeddings

Phase 3 – Knockout Stage
	•	Separate model head
	•	Penalize:
	•	Aggressive priors
	•	Boost:
	•	Draw probability
	•	Extra-time likelihood
	•	Unders

⸻

9️⃣ Markets to Prioritize for Monetization

World Cups are not about 1X2 only.

High-ROI Markets
	•	Under 2.5 / Under 3.5
	•	BTTS (No)
	•	Double Chance
	•	Draw No Bet
	•	Correct score bands (0–0, 1–0, 1–1)
	•	Qualification markets (to advance)

Perfect for WhatsApp delivery.

## Step 2 — Confirm odds join

Query the DB:


## Step 3 — Confirm training data source

The jump from **1370 → 5000** matches means:

Run:

```sql
SELECT COUNT(*)

LEFT JOIN odds_real_consensus orc ON tm.match_id = orc.match_id
```

* ~5k - 1370 = ~3630 matches with **no odds**.

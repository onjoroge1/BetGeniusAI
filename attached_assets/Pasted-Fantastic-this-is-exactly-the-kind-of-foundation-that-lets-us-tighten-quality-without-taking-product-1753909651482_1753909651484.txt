## Week‑by‑week plan (no user impact)

### **Week 1 — Historical odds value extraction (immediate lift)**

**A. Learn book weights (per league × era)**

* **Era binning:** 5‑year bins (e.g., 1993–97, 1998–02, …, 2019–23, 2024+) to get enough samples.
* **Metric:** out‑of‑time LogLoss per book using **closing implied probabilities** vs outcomes.
* **Weights:** solve a constrained optimization (non‑negative, sum‑to‑1). Two options:

  * **Simple**: softmax of negative LL per book (temperature‑scaled).
  * **Exact**: minimize LL of the **weighted consensus** on a validation era (scipy `SLSQP`), with `w≥0, Σw=1`.
* **Safeguards:** minimum coverage per book; fallback to equal‑weights if sparse.
* **Output:** `BOOK_WEIGHTS_{league}_{era}.csv`.

**B. Weighted T‑72 consensus (at feature time)**

* For each eval match at **T‑72**:

  * Compute margin‑adjusted 3‑way implied per book;
  * Combine with **learned weights** for that league/era;
  * Store `pH_cons_w`, `pD_cons_w`, `pA_cons_w`, plus **dispersion** (IQR/std) and `n_books`.
* This replaces the equal‑weight median we used before.

**C. Era & season priors**

* From historical odds: estimate **draw rate** and **home‑advantage** by **league × era**.
* Add **season‑phase** prior (early/mid/late) with **shrinkage** early in season (pool to era prior until N matches increases).
* Use priors **only** as inputs to the calibration layer and residual head (safe; no leakage).

**D. Verification (blockers)**

* Re‑run metrics **on the same evaluation slice** as your 0.8157 result:

  * Show LL/Brier/Top‑2 for **Market‑T72 (equal)** vs **Market‑T72 (weighted)** vs **Model (feature‑only)** vs **Model (residual‑on‑market)**.
* **Paired bootstrap CIs** for ΔLL (weighted vs equal; model vs weighted).
* **Horizon audit**: all snapshots used as features must satisfy `ts_snapshot ≤ KO−72h`.

## A few implementation nudges

* **Weights regularization:** temperature on the softmax or L2 on weights to avoid over‑fitting sparse eras.
* **Fallbacks:** if book coverage <2 or era sparse, use equal‑weights; record a flag in the features.
* **Probability caps:** clip inputs to logits in \[0.02, 0.98] to avoid infinities.
* **Era assignment at prediction time:** map current season to the nearest learned era; if outside range (e.g., 2025+), use most recent era.

2) Features to extract per match (T-72)

For every match at T-72, compute in a feature pack passed to the residual head:

2.1 Consensus-based inputs
	•	logit(q_cons_w): logit(pH_cons_w), logit(pD_cons_w), logit(pA_cons_w).
	•	Dispersion: dispH, dispD, dispA; n_books, avg_overround.
	•	Disagreement index: mean KL divergence across books vs consensus (or Jensen-Shannon); call it disagree_js.

2.2 Book-identity features (multi-book)

For each available book j:
	•	Delta-logits: Δlogit_j = logit(q_j) − logit(q_cons_w) for H/D/A (3 numbers).
(If many books: aggregate sharp vs rec means using bookmaker_meta.type.)
	•	Book presence mask: binary flags per book (or per group—sharp/rec).
	•	Book quality: inject quality_weight_j and ll_closing_j (league×era) so the model can learn to trust some books more even before weighting.
	•	Overround_j: predictive of book’s pricing style at T-72.

If you want a compact version:
	•	Aggregate sharp mean Δlogit and rec mean Δlogit; include their std.

2.3 Structural enrichment (existing)
	•	Team diffs (Elo/form/attack vs defense ratios), Poisson/DC λ features (λ_home, λ_away, λ_diff, λ_sum), rest/congestion, season-phase.

Missingness policy
	•	If a book is absent for a match: set deltas to 0 and present_flag=0 (or leave NaN + mask); never impute with consensus blindly without a mask.

⸻

3) Residual-on-Market head (book-aware)

We keep the same residual formulation but let the model learn from book signals:

\text{logit}(p_o) \;=\; \text{logit}(q^{\text{cons,\,weighted}}o)\;+\;g_o\big( x{\text{book}}, x_{\text{disp}}, x_{\text{struct}}\big),
\quad o\in\{H,D,A\}

Where g_o(\cdot) is a small CatBoost (or multinomial LR) that outputs delta logits. Then re-normalize and apply OOF per-league isotonic calibration.

Training details
	•	Walk-forward CV; optimize LogLoss.
	•	Sample weights (if any) from process scores only—not as features.
	•	Clip probabilities to [0.02, 0.98] before logits to avoid infinities.

⸻

4) New consensus formation (under the hood)

You already saw “equal vs optimal” perform similarly in Week 1. That’s common. The lift often comes when your residual head sees per-book deviations from the weighted consensus:
	•	Maintain weighted consensus at T-72 using book_quality_by_era weights.
	•	Let the residual head leverage Δlogit_j, overround_j, disagree_js, n_books to nudge away from consensus when multiple higher-quality books disagree in the same direction.

B. Did the 15-min filler produce snapshots?

       COUNT(DISTINCT match_id) matches_60m,

C. Do we have complete H/D/A triplets per match?

SELECT match_id,
       COUNT(*)                           AS rows,
       COUNT(DISTINCT book_id)            AS n_books,
       COUNT(DISTINCT outcome)            AS outcomes_present
FROM odds_snapshots
WHERE ts_snapshot > NOW() - INTERVAL '6 hours'
GROUP BY match_id
HAVING COUNT(DISTINCT outcome) = 3
ORDER BY n_books DESC
LIMIT 20;

D. Did we build consensus rows per bucket?

SELECT time_bucket, market, COUNT(*) matches
FROM consensus_predictions
WHERE created_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '24 hours'
GROUP BY time_bucket, market
ORDER BY time_bucket;

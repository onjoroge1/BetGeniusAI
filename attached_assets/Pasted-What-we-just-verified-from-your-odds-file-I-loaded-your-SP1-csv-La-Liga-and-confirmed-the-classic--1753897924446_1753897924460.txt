I computed margin‑adjusted implied probabilities per bookmaker and a consensus (median across books) for each match. Here’s what we’re doing for every row:

Raw implied: q_raw = [1/odd_H, 1/odd_D, 1/odd_A]

Overround: r = q_raw.sum()

Margin‑adjusted: q_adj = q_raw / r

Consensus: median of {q_adj^book} per outcome across available books; then re‑normalize so pH+pD+pA = 1.

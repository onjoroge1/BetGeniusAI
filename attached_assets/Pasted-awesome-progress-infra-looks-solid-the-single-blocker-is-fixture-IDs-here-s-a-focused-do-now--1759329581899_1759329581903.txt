```sql
  league_id INT,

  updated_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);
```

* Query fixtures with a **tight date window** (±1 day is already in your script) and **league+season** (you’ve implemented season logic).

  * exact canonical match against `apif_teams_ref.team_canonical`
  * else any alias hit from `team_alias`

